Enable your camera

Works over HTTPS or http://localhost. If you blocked the camera earlier, allow it in the site permissions and click Retry.

How to re-enable camera permission (Chrome / Safari / Firefox)
  • Chrome/Edge: Click the camera icon in the address bar → Allow → Reload.
  • Safari: Safari → Settings for This Website → Camera: Allow → Reload.
  • Firefox: Click the permissions icon (left of URL) → Allow camera → Reload.
